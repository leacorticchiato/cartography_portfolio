Academic Projects

WJCC Inequality: Housing, School Zoning, and Funding

Final project for Geovisualization & Cartography. This map overlays subsidized housing, middle school attendance zones, and per-pupil funding to visualize local inequality in Williamsburg–James City County (VA).

  • Tools: ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online
  • Techniques: choropleths, proportional symbols, custom legend & layout
View full-size map

Unicorn Habitat Suitability Study (StoryMap)

A playful but technically rigorous suitability analysis using terrain, land cover, and accessibility layers to model optimal unicorn habitat, presented as an interactive StoryMap.

  • Tools: ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS StoryMaps
  • Techniques: raster suitability modeling, reclassification, weighted overlay
Open Unicorn Habitat StoryMap

Wetland Loss & Land Cover Change in South Miami-Dade (StoryMap)

Remote sensing project tracking wetland disruption and quarry expansion between Florida City, the Everglades Outpost, and Bayfront Park from 2009–2023 using multi-temporal satellite imagery.

  • Tools: ArcGIS Pro / QGIS, ArcGIS StoryMaps
  • Techniques: NDVI, Tasseled Cap indices, change detection
Open Miami Wetlands StoryMap
